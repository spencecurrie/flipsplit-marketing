## Can I Sell My Home As-Is Legally?

Yes, absolutely. Selling your home as-is is entirely legal. But here’s the thing: as a seller, you’re still required to disclose known issues with the property. In California, for instance, you’ll need to complete a Transfer Disclosure Statement (TDS). This document outlines any known defects or material issues, from leaky roofs to foundation cracks. Buyers can (and often will) inspect the property. They’re entitled to understand what they’re purchasing. While selling as-is limits your repair obligations, transparency remains important.
